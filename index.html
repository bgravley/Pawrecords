YourPetPass — Your Dog's Health Records, Organized and Travel-Ready

YourPetPass is a pet health records and travel planning app built for dog and cat parents. It keeps vaccination history, vet visit records, allergies, and medications in one place — accessible no matter which veterinarian you see, in any city.

Built for pet parents who move

Whether you've switched vets, relocated, or travel frequently with your dog or cat, YourPetPass keeps every health record attached to your pet, not to a single clinic.

AI-powered travel checklists

Generate a route-specific checklist covering health certificates, vaccination requirements, and airline pet policies for both domestic and international travel. YourPetPass uses AI to research current requirements and flags anything that should be double-checked before you fly.

AI document scanning

Take a photo of any vet record, vaccine certificate, or health document and YourPetPass automatically extracts and saves the information to your pet's profile.

Emergency QR health card

Every pet gets a QR code linking to a public emergency health page — no login required — showing allergies, emergency contact info, and vaccination status. Useful for boarding, daycare, pet-sitters, or if a pet is ever lost.

Pricing

YourPetPass offers a free plan for core health record storage. Premium plans start at $4.99/month and include AI document scanning, AI travel checklists, weight tracking, document storage, and the QR emergency health card. Annual plans are $39.99/year and a Lifetime plan is available for $89.99.
